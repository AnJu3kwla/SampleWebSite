About the event

In this competition, five-member student teams representing all universities and Institutes of Sri Lanka will be competing with each other to prove their software development expertise. The best 15 teams of the first round will be competing in the final round for 12 competitive hours to be crowned as the winners. 

10 teams will be from Vavuniya Campus and 5 teams will be from other universities.


Events

Hackintra

Hackintra is an inter-university 12 hours overnight hackathon and biggest event in this events series. Teams will be selected from top teams in HackaDay.

CodeZpark

Codespark is a pre-hack event to make some motivation among the students to participate in hackintra. It is a Coding challenge where students will be able to use any language to solve given problems.

Twists N' Turns

Twists N' Turns is a technical workshop that will elaborate on the newest technologies in information technology and will discuss the trends in the industry. These sessions are conducted by many industry experts.

Esports Championship

Exports Championship is the only event related to esports happens in the nothern region. Any person from the Vavuniya Campus can participate in this event and can be titled as Esports champions.

Rules & Regulations

Each team can consist of a maximum of 5 members and not less than 3 members from the same university/institute any University or Higher Educational Institute.


There is no limitation in using technologies, A team can use any technology and trends available.


Each team from Vavuniya Campus have to compete in internal mini hackathon limited to six hours. 


Each team should provide a video describing their idea and should be limited to 1 minute.


Your idea should be your own and fresh, ideas which have been already developed for other purposes will not be eligible to enter.


Proposed Idea cannot be changed. Final product may have added features.
